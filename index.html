Organization Divisions

GENESIS consists of five complementary divisions that develop students' journalism skills.

Article & News Writing

Focus on news reporting, features, opinions, and clear, ethical writing.

Photography

Capture journalistic moments with strong visual storytelling and ethics.

Design & Layout

Design magazine layouts, posters, and digital publications with professional composition.

Social Media & Publication

Manage online presence and publish content across social platforms responsibly.

Editorial & Redaction

Responsible for editing, fact-checking, and final publishing standards.

GENESIS Code of Ethics

  1. Uphold truth and honesty in every publication.
  2. Avoid plagiarism in any form.
  3. Respect the privacy of interviewees and sources.
  4. Write objectively and responsibly.
  5. Maintain the good reputation of the organization and the school.
